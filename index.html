Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

    <1i>2 eggs <1i>2tbs butter <1i>2tbs cream

    Method

    <1i>Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat <1i>Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl <1i>Once butter has melted add cream and eggs the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making and omelette) <1i>When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
